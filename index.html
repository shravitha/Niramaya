About Alva's Niraamaya

Alva’s Niraamaya is a multi-specialty AYUSH hospital functioning under the aegis of Alva’s Education Foundation. Established in 1996 by the great visionary Dr. M. Mohan Alva, Alva’s Ayurveda Medical College marks the first stepping stone laid by this exemplar.

With a vision towards healthcare for every strata of society, a new unit, Alva’s Niraamaya was established. The word ‘Aamaya' means ‘a disease or an affliction'. The word Niraamaya signifies the state of being free from ‘Aamaya' in other words free from disease. At Niraamaya, our endeavor is to imbibe the essence of AYUSH systems of medicine. Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Homeopathy systems of medicine including Yoga are incorporated to treat the ailing and/or rejuvenating/maintaining the physical and mental wellbeing of the individual.

Services

Ayurveda

Treatment and prevention of disease by following a healthy life style is basic objectives mentioned in Ayurveda treatises to attain and sustain health through the old age. Swasthya (health) is defined as someone whose mind-body constitution are all in equilibrium, the digestive fire, in addition to the body’s tissues and waste products being in balance.

Naturopathy

Naturopathy is a system of treating, preventing, curing diseases with natural care that comes from natural sources. It focus on body's capacity to heal itself. Naturopathic treatment plans focus on education and prevention. They often emphasize diet, exercise, and stress management.

Homeopathy

Discovered in early 1800's by a German physician, Samuel Hahnemann, Homeopathy seeks to cure in accordance with natural laws of healing and use medicines made from natural sources.Homeopathy claims to stimulate healing responses to diseases by administering substances that mimic the symptoms of those diseases in healthy people.

Panchakarma

Panchakarma Treatment is a specialized Ayurvedic therapy that involves the internal and external purification of the body through specially designed procedures that brings equilibrium of the doshas and dhatus in the body. Thus, it alleviates the disease condition and improvises the health in healthy individuals.

Yoga

Yoga is an ancient practise that aims to bring balance and health to an individual's physical, mental, emotional. Yoga sessions typically involve a combination of physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation. Yoga improves strength, balance and flexibility. We offer both therapeutic and general yoga.to an individual’s physical, mental and emotional states

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy involves the holistic approach to the prevention, diagnosis and therapeutic management of pain, disorders of movement or optimisation of function to enhance the health and welfare of the community from an individual or population perspective. Physiotherapy helps to restore movement and function when someone is affected by injury, illness or disability.
